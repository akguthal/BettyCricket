#SquishingTheStigma

About Us

We are Betty Cricket and we are trying to make insects a mainstream food source. We are freshmen at the University of Maryland College Park of different majors including Engineering, Computer Science and Elementary Education. As a part of the Entrepreneurship and Innovation program we are participating in the Do Good Challenge, which is an 8 week competition where teams create projects or ventures for social good.

Why Crickets?

Crickets are an alternative and more sustainable source of protein.

It takes 2000 times more water to produce a pound of beef than a pound of crickets.

Our goal is to remove the social stigma surrounding edible insects.

Our Partners

Cricket Flours is a start-up company dedicated to producing cricket-based foods including flour, protein powder, and soon brownie mix. We partnered with CEO Charles Wilson in order to bring these healthy and sustainable products to UMD students. In return, we provide them with advertisement to a large market and spread the word about sustainability.
